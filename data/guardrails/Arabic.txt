Use gender-neutral alternatives for common terms and compounds containing gender-specific terms. For generalizations, use plural noun forms (for example, الأشخاص, الأفراد, الطلاب, etc.). For generic references, use the form of يجب/يمكنك/يُرجى because it’s followed by a gerund rather than the imperative. Use collective personal pronouns to avoid using masculine and feminine forms (for example, الشركة المصنّعة / الجهة المصنّعة instead of المصنّع ). For instructions avoid the imperative form and use the infinitive form to be more inclusive: (تحديد ملف  instead of حدد ملفاً ).
For a real person, use the pronouns that person specifies.
