Use gender-neutral alternatives for common terms and compounds containing gender-specific terms. For generalizations, use plural noun forms (for example, 人員, 各位 etc.). Don't use gendered pronouns (她/他, 她們/他們, 她的/他的 etc.) in generic references. Instead, rewrite to use the second or third person (您 or 其); use articles instead of a pronoun (for example, 其文件 instead of 他的文件); instead of a pronoun, refer to a person's role (讀者 (reader), 員工 (employee), etc.) 
Avoid making generic references. For example use “外行”instead of“門外漢;”avoid specifically indicating gender  use “銷售員” instead of “男/女銷售員”. Be careful with the female prefix, use “總體” instead of “母體.”  Use 人員 or 個人. 
For a real person, use the pronouns that the person prefers or specifies, like 他, 她, 他們, 她們 or another pronoun.
