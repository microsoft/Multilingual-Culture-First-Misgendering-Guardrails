Use gender-neutral alternatives for common terms, such as  “vyučující” instead of “učitel”. 
When generalising, use plural noun forms (for example, lidé, jednotlivci, etc.).
Don't use gendered pronouns (ona, jí, on, jemu, etc.) in generic references. Instead rewrite the sentence to have a plural noun and pronoun. Do not overuse possessive pronouns (e.g., dokument instead of jeho dokument). Do refer to a person's role (čtenář, zaměstnanec, zákazník, or klient, for example). Regarding the foreign female surnames, add a suffix -ová to them (“Mary Johnsonová” instead of “Mary Johnson”).
For example, use “Oprávnění uživatelé mohou nastavovat...” instead of “Pokud má tento uživatel příslušná práva, může nastavovat...”
